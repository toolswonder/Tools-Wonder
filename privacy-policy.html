Privacy Policy

At Tools Wonder, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that are collected and recorded by Tools Wonder and how we use it.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment details. All tools on our website are designed to be used anonymously, directly in your browser.

2. Usage Data

We may collect non-personal data related to how users interact with our website to improve functionality and user experience. This may include: Browser type and version Operating system Device type (desktop/mobile) Referring website Pages visited and time spent on each Error logs (if any) This information is collected in an aggregated and anonymous form and does not identify any individual user.

3. Cookies

Tools Wonder does not use cookies for tracking personal information. However, some third-party tools or plugins embedded in our site may use cookies. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Some tools on our website may rely on third-party APIs or services. These third parties may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services for more details. We do not share any user data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

>

Our website uses GitHub Pages and relies on HTTPS to secure data transmission. Since no personal data is collected or stored, the risk of data breaches is minimal. However, we encourage users to avoid uploading sensitive or confidential files.

6. Children's Privacy

Tools Wonder is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. We encourage users to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your experience with our website, feel free to contact us via our GitHub page or by opening an issue.
